Hullo

I’m the digital editor for the NBC Bay Area Investigative Unit. I create data-driven interactives like maps, charts and other digital stuff.

I’m also a radio journalist and used to be the content director for KBIA Public Radio. I covered tech and business and hosted a podcast called “CoMo Explained.”

While at KBIA I started and ran a program called the Missouri Drone Journalism Program in 2012. A lot has changed since then, but they’re still working on the possibilites of drone photography in journalism. Contact Matthew Dickinson for more information on what they’re doing now.
