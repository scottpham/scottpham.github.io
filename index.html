Hullo

I'm Scott Pham, the digital editor for the NBC Bay Area Investigative Unit. I report on data-centric stories and create interactive visualizations like maps, charts and other digital stuff.

I'm also a radio journalist and used to be the content director for KBIA Public Radio. I covered tech and business and hosted a podcast called CoMo Explained."

While at KBIA, I co-founded and ran a program called the Missouri Drone Journalism Program in 2012. I've since left the radio station, but the University of Missouri is still exploring the field of drone journalism. Contact Matthew Dickinson for more information on what they're doing now.
